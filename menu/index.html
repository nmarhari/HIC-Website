Our Lodging

Our lodgings at Giovanni's are a sanctuary of comfort and serenity, where the beauty of nature meets the luxury of modern living. Each of our accommodations is thoughtfully designed to immerse you in the wilderness while providing all the modern amenities you need. Whether you choose a cozy cabin nestled among the trees, a lakeside villa with breathtaking views, or a rustic-chic lodge room, expect tasteful decor, plush furnishings, and a soothing ambiance.
