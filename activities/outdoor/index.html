Outdoor Activities

Fishing

Fish with the family at one of our many fishing spots at Giovanni's Pond. Fishing rods are available for rental!

Trails

Explore many of our hiking/biking trails within the wilderness. Each trail is marked and mapped to insure safety and correct navigation.

Canoeing and Paddle Boarding

Canoes and Paddleboards available for rental at Giovanni's pond! Open area and plenty of space for group sessions. Canoe lessons are available!

Sports

Giovanni's Lodge has many outdoor sports complexes! Tennis courts and basketball courts are located on the east side of the pavilion. The soccer field and pool are located on the west side!
